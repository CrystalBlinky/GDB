Campus Life

Library

It is the school library. Students from mostly all classes are allowed to read books of their choice in their library period. Students can issue books for a maximum period of 1 week if needed.



Playground

A playground has been built in both the senior and the junior building. The junior building playground has a basketball playing court and some rides like a swing, a slide, etc. But the senior building playground is quite large compared to the junior building one. Enough space to play any outdoor games.



Halls

There are different types of halls in this school. The school has a sports hall which is needed when it rains outside. They have a music hall where they teach students to sing. A movie hall for keeping the students entertained by making them watch good movies. A assembly hall for different assemblies. With studying, this school provides a facility for students to also do extra co-curricular activities. Students get chance to participate in assemblies and enough time to be fully prepared for their performance. In junior building, the teachers host the assemblies but in senior building, students get chance to host.
